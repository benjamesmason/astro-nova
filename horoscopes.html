Your Weekly Horoscope

Aries

WEEK OF September 4 - 1 0, 2023

Keep those proverbial purse strings tight! Money might start to get a little funny (but not necessarily amusing) this Monday, September 4. That day, Jupiter, which is lucky and expansive in forward motion, shifts into his annual four-month retrograde, this year in your financial sector. This isn’t cause to hit the panic button, however, Ram. Think about it the way an economist would: as a natural “correction,” meaning getting things back on a realistic keel after some carefree spending. If you’ve been living like there’s no tomorrow—or conveniently forgetting that you (at least theoretically) have a budget—this U-turn may be a welcome event. No one expects your freewheeling sign to give up all your luxuries, but you may learn new ways to indulge your high-end tastes that are more resourceful than paying full retail. While wise Jupiter is back-spinning, you might come to enjoy sample sales and upscale consignment shops. To stay motivated during this short-term belt-tightening cycle (through December 30), identify a few big-ticket goals for yourself, from saving up for a new set of wheels next year to a winter vacation to a down payment on a home.


While you’re experimenting with new systems and processes, Aries, consider asking for help when it’s needed and not just resorting to “doing it all by my damn self.” Wednesday’s quarter moon in cooperative Gemini shines its balancing beams on your one-Ram show and reminds you that you’ll go farther faster with a little help from your friends. There are potential collaborators who would make life easier (and more profitable)—but you’d have to give them a chance to show you! This may be the moment where you lower the steel gates enough to let someone you’ve known on a casual level become a bona fide project partner—or a true friend. On the other hand, you may realize that a certain creative partnership effort has hit the point of diminishing returns. Heed this even-keeled quarter moon and treat them the way you’d want to be treated (i.e., don’t just blow them off). But before you give up the ghost, why not sit down for an honest talk and see if anything can be salvaged or improved upon. If not, at least you can move on with positive regard for each other.


If you’re not already in the habit of performing random acts of kindness, you might want to start this Friday, when generous Jupiter (retrograde) in Taurus syncs up with the Virgo Sun in your sixth house of service. Even when it’s back-pedaling, Jupiter invites abundance and luck—just with warnings to stay realistic. Under these warm-hearted beams, it can literally pay to pay it forward. Lend a hand to a co-worker who’s clearly overburdened; hold the door for someone carrying a load—or offer to help. No quid pro quos, either. When you give freely, without expectation, you show people (and the universe) what you’re made of. As you probably know, true wealth is not measured by how much money you have in the bank but by the richness of the relationships you have in your life.

This isn’t cause to hit the panic button, however, Ram. Think about it the way an economist would: as a natural “correction,” meaning getting things back on a realistic keel after some carefree spending. If you’ve been living like there’s no tomorrow—or conveniently forgetting that you (at least theoretically) have a budget—this U-turn may be a welcome event. No one expects your freewheeling sign to give up all your luxuries, but you may learn new ways to indulge your high-end tastes that are more resourceful than paying full retail. While wise Jupiter is back-spinning, you might come to enjoy sample sales and upscale consignment shops. To stay motivated during this short-term belt-tightening cycle (through December 30), identify a few big-ticket goals for yourself, from saving up for a new set of wheels next year to a winter vacation to a down payment on a home.


While you’re experimenting with new systems and processes, Aries, consider asking for help when it’s needed and not just resorting to “doing it all by my damn self.” Wednesday’s quarter moon in cooperative Gemini shines its balancing beams on your one-Ram show and reminds you that you’ll go farther faster with a little help from your friends. There are potential collaborators who would make life easier (and more profitable)—but you’d have to give them a chance to show you! This may be the moment where you lower the steel gates enough to let someone you’ve known on a casual level become a bona fide project partner—or a true friend. On the other hand, you may realize that a certain creative partnership effort has hit the point of diminishing returns. Heed this even-keeled quarter moon and treat them the way you’d want to be treated (i.e., don’t just blow them off). But before you give up the ghost, why not sit down for an honest talk and see if anything can be salvaged or improved upon. If not, at least you can move on with positive regard for each other.


If you’re not already in the habit of performing random acts of kindness, you might want to start this Friday, when generous Jupiter (retrograde) in Taurus syncs up with the Virgo Sun in your sixth house of service. Even when it’s back-pedaling, Jupiter invites abundance and luck—just with warnings to stay realistic. Under these warm-hearted beams, it can literally pay to pay it forward. Lend a hand to a co-worker who’s clearly overburdened; hold the door for someone carrying a load—or offer to help. No quid pro quos, either. When you give freely, without expectation, you show people (and the universe) what you’re made of. As you probably know, true wealth is not measured by how much money you have in the bank but by the richness of the relationships you have in your life.
